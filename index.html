About Love Match

Answer Questions

Your answers are only seen by the algorithm and only minimal information is sent to your match about you.

Love Algorithm

The love algorithm uses the pool of results and your personal preferences to create one-to-one matches.

Get Results

You and your match will recieved an email with your match type, each other's email, age, and social media.

Types of Matches

The algorithm finds you one of three types of matches based on the pool of potentials.

Love Match

A love match means that you and your match are a perfect match. The main goal of the algorithm is to find everyone a one-to-one love match. Although it prioritizes that, this may not be the case for every single match that it creates. In that case, it pairs the remaining pool with either a compatible match or a friend match.

Compatible Match

A compatible match means that you and your match have minor differences but they small enough for there to be compatibility. It may not be a love match but that doesn't mean there isn't anything there to explore!

Friend Match

A friend match means there were major differences between you and your match for there to be romantic compatibility. However, this match could be the start of a beautiful friendship.
